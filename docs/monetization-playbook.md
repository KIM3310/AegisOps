# Monetization Playbook - AegisOps

This file is a commercial planning artifact, not a guarantee of revenue. Use it to make the repository legible as a sellable product, pilot, workshop, license, or proof-of-value package.

## Commercial Lane

- **Lane:** B2B incident operations cockpit
- **Primary buyer:** SOC leads, IT operations managers, MSP incident teams, and infrastructure owners with noisy handoffs.
- **Revenue wedge:** A replayable incident review cockpit that turns logs and screenshots into structured handoff evidence.

## Offer Ladder

1. **Incident-review tabletop package** - pricing hypothesis: $2k-$5k tabletop workshop
2. **Managed replay/eval setup for customer incidents** - pricing hypothesis: $8k-$20k implementation pilot
3. **Monthly incident-quality scorecard and operator handoff review** - pricing hypothesis: $1.5k-$6k/month managed review support

## Acquisition Channels

- LinkedIn demos for SOC/MSP leaders
- Incident postmortem templates distributed as lead magnets
- Cloudflare demo plus 20-minute buyer walkthrough

## Proof Gates Before Selling

- Replay eval suite passes
- Review smoke script passes
- Demo mode works without provider keys

## Risk Boundaries

- No production tenant claims without integration
- Provider keys stay server-side only
- Customer logs require retention and redaction policy

## 30/60/90 Day GTM

- **30 days:** Ship a 3-scenario public demo clip
- **60 days:** Create MSP/SOC discovery checklist
- **90 days:** Offer 3 pilot slots with fixed success criteria

## Metrics That Matter

- Time-to-report reduction
- Handoff completeness score
- Replay eval pass rate

## Positioning Note

Lead with the specific operational pain, then show the runnable proof, then offer a narrow pilot. Do not pitch the repository as a vague app; pitch it as a concrete workflow with evidence, risk boundaries, and a next buying step.
